## 🚀 About Us

**Enviros Group** is a global leader in providing integrated, data-driven insights and innovative engineering solutions that support the global energy transition. We focus on delivering advanced technology and digital solutions across challenging offshore and onshore environments, ensuring safety and sustainability for our clients' projects.

## 🛠️ Key Services & Expertise

Our comprehensive suite of geosciences and environmental consultancy services are organized into several key divisions:

- 🌊 **Geophysical Surveys:** High-resolution marine mapping and hazard identification.
- 🏗️ **Geotechnical Services:** Comprehensive investigations for offshore, nearshore, and onshore projects.
- 🌱 **Environmental & Metocean:** Environmental baseline studies, permitting, and meteorological/oceanographic data collection.
- ⚙️ **Asset Integrity:** Specialized monitoring and maintenance for marine and energy infrastructure.
- 🗺️ **Geomatics & Seismic:** Advanced spatial data management and 2D/3D seismic data acquisition.
- 🛢️ **Oilfield & Subsea Services:** Technical support for well intervention and subsea operations.

## 🌐 Industries Served

We operate across five primary sectors:

1. 💨 **Renewables:** Primarily focused on Offshore Wind development.
2. 🛢️ **Oil & Gas:** Supporting the entire lifecycle from exploration to decommissioning.
3. 🌉 **Infrastructure:** Marine and coastal civil engineering support.
4. 🔌 **Subsea Cables:** Route surveys and installation support for international interconnectors.
5. ⛏️ **Deep-Sea Mining:** Environmental and technical studies for the emerging mineral exploration sector.

## 🌍 Global Presence

Operating globally with a strong presence in **Europe, Africa, the Americas, the Middle East, and Asia Pacific**. 

Our specialized partner companies include:
- **HGIS:** Specialists in hydrographic and geophysical surveys.
- **Alpine Ocean Seismic Survey:** A leading provider of marine data collection in the Americas.
- **Lighthouse Geo:** Focused on high-end marine survey and positioning.
